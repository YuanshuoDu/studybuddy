What it is

Three things, done well.

No newsfeed. No algorithm. No infinite scroll. Just a map, a list, and a tap to show up.

📍

Map

Mapbox-powered map shows what's happening within 5–50 km of you right now. Filter by type, time, school, women-only, free.

🤝

Match

Sign up in two taps. Hosts review pending requests, the platform handles the rest. Re-signup is supported — cancellations never double-count.

⭐

Meet

Check in with a 6-digit code, meet your people, rate each other afterwards. 1–5 stars + tags + a 500-char comment (private by default).

How it works

From "I'm bored" to "see you Saturday" in four steps.

Sign up

WeChat, phone, or Apple ID. Pick your school, major, and enrollment year. Done.

Discover

Map view by default. Drag the radius, filter by what you like. Tap anything to see who's already in.

Sign up

One tap. Optional one-liner to the host. Approval required only for women-only or paid activities.

Show up

Enter the 6-digit code at the door. Rate each other afterwards. That's it.

FAQ

Five quick answers.

The full 10-question FAQ (with screenshots) is in the repo.

Is it free?
Yes — Pairhub v1.0 is completely free. The platform charges no service fee and takes no commission. Whether the activity itself charges is up to the host; the activity detail page says so explicitly. We provide AA-split / WeChat-pay tools but never touch the money.
Do you sell my data?
No. We don't sell, rent, or trade your data. We don't run ad SDKs. We don't embed any third-party analytics or tracking scripts. Your data lives in AWS Frankfurt (eu-central-1) with us-east-1 standby, encrypted at rest with AES-256. See privacy policy.
How does the safety check work?
Two gates: (1) auto content-safety via WeChat / Aliyun API on title + description + cover image, (2) human second-pass on a random 1% of auto-passed activities. Auto moderation only sees the 3 content fields — never your phone, openid, IP, or device ID. See mp-audit-config.
Can I delete my account?
Yes — in-app, one tap. After a 7-day cooling-off period the account is fully deleted (irrecoverable). Before deletion you can one-click export all your personal data as JSON, sent to your registered email within 7 days.
Does it work outside China?
Yes — Pairhub is built for Chinese students studying abroad (US, UK, EU, AU, CA, SG, JP, etc.). The map uses Mapbox with WGS-84 (international standard) — no GCJ-02 offset. Push notifications use the regional best-fit (APNs / FCM / WeChat template / TPNS for mainland).
